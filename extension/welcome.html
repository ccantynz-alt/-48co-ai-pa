Voice punctuation & commands

Say punctuation naturally. 48co converts it for you.

“period” / “comma” / “question mark” Inserts punctuation
“new line” / “new paragraph” Line breaks
“open paren” / “close paren” Brackets
“thumbs up emoji” Inserts emoji
“refactor this” / “debug this” Dev prompts
“send it” Submits message
“cancel” Clears & resets
